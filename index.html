Dual-IMU Cancellation

Measures relative tilt between ear (0x68) and neck C7 (0x69). A complementary filter (α=0.90 gyro, α=0.10 accel) cancels out full-body lean, preventing false alerts.

4-State Haptic Escalation

When angle > 15°, a 2N2222-driven coin motor escalates haptic patterns based on duration: WARN (0s) → GENTLE (10s) → STRONG (30s) → CRITICAL (60s).
